Introduction

I am a CIFRE PhD student working with the Noah's Ark team at Huawei Technologies France and the LIPADE lab at Paris Descartes University, co-advised by Ievgen Redko (Huawei) , Themis Palpanas (LIPADE) and Hassine Moungla . My research primarily focuses on time series analysis, especially in classification and forecasting. In my thesis's early stages, I explored data augmentation and synthetic generation methods for time series, tackling high labeling and cleaning costs. I then expanded my research to include adversarial attacks, developing innovative attack frameworks and adaptive defense mechanisms. Currently, I am also working on time series forecasting, transport optimal theory, and influence functions for data selection. My work, while centered on time series classification and forecasting, can be readily adapted to other fields like computer vision or NLP. Previously, I earned an engineering diploma in Data Science, Statistics and Learning from ENSAE and a Data Science Research Master degree from Ecole Polytechnique.
